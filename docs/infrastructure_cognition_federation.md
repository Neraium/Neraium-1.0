# Infrastructure Cognition Federation

Infrastructure cognition federation supports cross-organization structural knowledge sharing without raw telemetry exchange.

## Federated artifacts
- Validated archetypes
- Replay summaries
- Convergence models
- Ontology contributions
- Behavioral taxonomy mappings
- Structural evolution rules
- Evidence summaries

## Federation guarantees
- Privacy-preserving payloads
- Provenance and auditability
- Validation and governance traceability
