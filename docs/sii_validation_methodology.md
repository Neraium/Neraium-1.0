# SII Validation Methodology

SII validation does not use predictive maintenance metrics.

## Validated Dimensions

- earliest topology divergence visibility
- propagation visibility lead time
- subsystem fragmentation detection timing
- replay fidelity and continuity
- evidence consistency and lineage completeness
- continuation pathway coherence
- convergence tracking quality
- archetype emergence timing

## Validation Outcomes

- structural visibility timing
- evidence integrity level
- replay consistency level
- subsystem agreement level
- cognition continuity level
- topology coherence tracking status
