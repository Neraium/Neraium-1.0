# Infrastructure Behavior Science

Neraium’s Infrastructure Behavior Science layer formalizes how physical infrastructure structurally evolves over time.

It studies topology change, propagation emergence, subsystem fragmentation, recovery convergence, and operator intervention effects through replay-backed evidence.

## Principles
- Evidence-backed structural interpretation
- Replayable and auditable cognition outputs
- Non-actuating read-only boundaries
- Non-deterministic structural tendencies, not deterministic failure claims
