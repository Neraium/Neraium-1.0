# SII Operational Reasoning Simulation

Operational reasoning simulation explores structural continuation alternatives without deterministic failure prediction.

## Simulator scope
`backend/simulation/operational_reasoning_simulator.py` supports:
- propagation suppression scenarios
- subsystem reconvergence scenarios
- environmental pressure shifts
- load redistribution conditions
- compensation breakdown pathways
- recovery convergence alternatives
- intervention timing impacts

## Output requirements
- Replayable simulation frames
- Continuation envelopes
- Convergence pathways
- Evidence assumptions per scenario

## Governance
- No deterministic failure timestamps.
- No autonomous recommendation dispatch.
- All scenarios must expose assumption and evidence basis.
