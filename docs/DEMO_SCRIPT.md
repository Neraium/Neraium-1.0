# Neraium Demo Script

Use this as the short operator/investor demo path. Keep the demo focused on system intelligence, not a single vertical.

## One Sentence

Neraium helps teams see system drift before it becomes operational failure by turning telemetry into evidence, replay, and operator-ready next steps.

## Two Minute Demo Flow

### 1. Start with the problem

Complex systems usually fail before humans can see the pattern. Operators get dashboards, alarms, and raw telemetry, but they often do not get a clear answer to what changed, why it matters, and what to check next.

### 2. Show the system status

Open the main system view and point out that Neraium is looking at system behavior, not just isolated sensor thresholds.

Say:

> This is not another threshold alarm dashboard. Neraium looks at how signals move together and whether the system is drifting from its normal operating behavior.

### 3. Upload telemetry

Open Upload Data and upload a telemetry export.

Call out:

- upload validation
- large export handling
- worker status visibility
- background processing
- persisted job status
- replay artifact generation

Say:

> Operators can start with the data they already have. Neraium validates it, processes it, and turns the upload into an evidence-backed system result.

### 4. Show the finding

Open the findings or operator report view.

Call out:

- current system state
- confidence
- why it matters
- supporting evidence
- review next
- data quality limitations

Say:

> The product is designed to answer the operator's real questions: what changed, what evidence supports it, and what should I inspect next?

### 5. Show replay

Open Evidence Replay.

Call out:

- before/current/latest moments
- frame-based progression
- supporting variables
- replay as traceability, not animation for show

Say:

> Replay gives the operator and technical team a way to trace how the behavior changed over time instead of trusting a black-box score.

### 6. Close with platform scope

Say:

> The same core system intelligence layer can apply across facilities, equipment, water systems, energy systems, building automation, industrial operations, and other telemetry-heavy environments.

## Thirty Second Version

Neraium turns telemetry into operator-ready system intelligence. It detects drift, validates the data, generates evidence, and gives teams a replayable explanation of what changed and what to inspect next. The current platform is read-only and built for complex operational environments where relationships across signals matter more than isolated threshold alarms.

## What Not To Say

Avoid saying Neraium is only for cultivation, only for one industry, or an equipment controller. The current product is a read-only intelligence layer for complex systems.

Avoid saying the platform predicts everything. The stronger claim is that Neraium detects system drift earlier and explains the evidence behind the finding.

Avoid calling it an AI dashboard. The product is a system intelligence platform with deterministic SII outputs, evidence, replay, auditability, and operator workflow.
