# SII Operator Cognition Training

Operator cognition training uses replay and simulation to strengthen human interpretation of structural evolution.

## Training modules
- Identify relationship decay
- Recognize compensation masking
- Inspect propagation pathways
- Interpret evidence lineage
- Compare structural memory matches
- Evaluate recovery convergence
- Understand continuation windows

## Outputs
- Training scenarios
- Training replay artifacts
- Operator assessments
- Cognition training progress

## Boundary
Training supports human judgment and does not automate operator decisions.
